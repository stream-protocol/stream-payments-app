## Using StreamPay

1. **Choose StreamPay**: On the checkout page, select StreamPay as your payment method.

2. **Connect Wallet**: You'll be automatically prompted to connect your StreamPay / Solana wallet.

3. **Approve Payment**: In the StreamPaymentsâ„¢ interface, confirm the transaction.

4. **Confirm Payment**: Check the transaction history in your wallet for confirmation.

### Troubleshooting

-   **Failed Payments**: Error messages will be provided for issues such as insufficient funds, cancellations, or technical problems.

-   **Refunds**: Contact the merchant directly for refunds.

-   **Support**: For assistance, email commerce@streampayments.org

### Additional Info

-   **Fees**: Transactions have minimal fees (less than $0.001).

-   **Product Availability**: Availability of StreamPay depends on the merchant's settings.
