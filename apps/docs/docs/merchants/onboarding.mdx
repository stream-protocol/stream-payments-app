## Install StreamPay on Shopify

1. Navigate to the [Shopify StreamPay App Installation Link](https://apps.shopify.com/stream-pay) on the Shopify App Store and click the “Add app” button.

## Configure StreamPayments Portal™

1. Enter in your business information. It can take up to 48 hours to be verified by our team.
2. Enter in your StreamPay or Solana wallet address. This is where you will receive payments from your customers.
3. Review and accept the [Privacy Policy](https://merchant.streampayments.app/privacy) and [Terms of Service](https://merchant.streampayments.app/termsofservice).
